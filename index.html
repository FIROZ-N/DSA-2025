About — Straight talk

I get paid to ask three questions: What happened? Why did it happen? What do we do next? I take raw business data, clean it, and give decision-makers the insights they need to act. I prefer clear metrics over vague dashboards and I push for reproducibility: every analysis should be re-runnable and explainable.

What I do

  • Data cleaning & wrangling with Python (pandas) and SQL
  • Analysis & hypothesis testing to answer product questions
  • Interactive dashboards and executive reports (Tableau / Power BI)
  • Automation for repeatable reporting using scripts and queries

What I won't do

I won't build a dashboard that looks pretty but answers nothing. Design without metrics is decoration; I focus on business outcomes and measurable impact.
